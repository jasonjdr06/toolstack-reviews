## 4. Stable Diffusion — Best Free and Open Source

**Best for:** Budget-conscious creators, developers, open-source enthusiasts

Stable Diffusion democratizes AI image generation by being free and open-source. Run it locally on your own GPU, use it through free web interfaces, or leverage commercial services. The image quality is respectable—better for some styles, different strengths than Midjourney. The customization is exceptional: you can fine-tune models, use custom LoRAs, adjust every parameter.

For developers and technically inclined users, Stable Diffusion's flexibility is unmatched. The community has created thousands of tools, integrations, and workflows. Models like DreamShaper, Deliberate, and RealisticVision push image quality higher. It's the backbone of many commercial AI image services.

**Pricing:** Free (open-source)
**Free trial:** Fully free, no limits
