## 3. Ahrefs — Best for Backlink Analysis

**Best for:** Competitive analysis, link building, SEO research

Ahrefs has built the largest link database in SEO. If you need to understand backlink profiles, find link building opportunities, analyze competitor links, or monitor your own link growth, Ahrefs is the specialist. The backlink data is extraordinarily detailed—you can see link quality, anchor text, target pages, and more.

Beyond links, Ahrefs provides comprehensive keyword research, rank tracking, and content insights. The "Content Gap" feature identifies topics competitors rank for but you don't. Site Explorer shows where traffic comes from. The AI-powered recommendations surface opportunities others miss.
